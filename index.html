🛡️ Warranty

At our store, we stand behind the quality and performance of every product we sell. Whether you're purchasing a custom-built PC or individual components, your satisfaction and reliability are our top priorities.

🖥️
BUILT PCs

All pre-built and custom-built computers come with a hardware warranty covering manufacturing defects and hardware failures under normal use. Each system is carefully assembled and tested before delivery to ensure optimal performance and stability.

If a hardware issue occurs within the warranty period, our team will inspect, repair, or replace the defective component to get your system running again as quickly as possible.

⚙️
COMPONENTS

All individual components such as graphics cards, processors, motherboards, RAM, storage drives, and power supplies include a warranty provided by their respective manufacturers. Warranty length may vary depending on the product and brand.

In case of a defect, we will assist you through the warranty process and help you resolve the issue efficiently.

❌
WHAT IS NOT COVERED

The warranty does not cover damages caused by:

  • Physical damage or accidental misuse
  • Improper installation or modification
  • Liquid damage
  • Unauthorized repairs or tampering
💬
WARRANTY SUPPORT

If you experience any issues with your product, please contact our support team with your order number and product details. Our technicians will guide you through the troubleshooting process and provide the best solution possible.

Your trust matters to us, and we are committed to ensuring that every customer receives reliable products and dependable support.

Our support team is ready to assist you. Reach out anytime.
